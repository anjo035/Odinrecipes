Christmas Morning Hot Chocolate

Stir up this quick and easy hot chocolate recipe for a cozy hot drink sweetened with sugar and condensed milk on Christmas morning.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk
  • ½ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • ¼ cup instant hot cocoa mix
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan over medium heat for 3 minutes. Pour in condensed milk, hot chocolate, sugar, and vanilla extract; stir constantly until the mixture is warmed through and the sugar is dissolved, about 3 minutes.

Nutrition Facts

307Calories 9g Fat 45g Carbs 12g Protein

Cook's Note

If you don't think the hot chocolate is chocolaty enough, add 1 teaspoon of unsweetened cocoa powder.
